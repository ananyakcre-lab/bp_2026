Antibiotic Awareness Simulation

Welcome to this interactive simulation designed to educate users about the importance of responsible antibiotic use and the consequences of antibiotic resistance. In this simulation, you will navigate through various scenarios that mimic real-life situations. You will make decisions on whether to take antibiotics or not, and see how those decisions impact your health and the broader community over time. Through this experience, you will gain a deeper understanding of how antibiotic resistance develops and why it is crucial to use antibiotics wisely to preserve their effectiveness for future generations.
